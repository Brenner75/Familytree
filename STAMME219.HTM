Scholz Maria was born in 1825 in Österreich. Österreich (Austria) is a state in the heart of Europe with 8.8 Mio inhabitants.

She was married to Scholz Karl -, 26 years older -, a_glove maker, born in 1820 from Wien. She was a_washing woman and the couple lived in Wien. They had 5 children: Elisabeth, born in 1845, Theresia, born in 1849, Karl, born in 1846, Anna, born in 1847 and Wilhelm, born in 1850.

The exact date of death is not known, but she died propably at the age of 84 years in the year 1909.

She lived in the epoch of Classicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Ferdinand I of Habsburg-Lothringen. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Sardinian-Austrian war, the Hungarian Revolution and the Sardinian war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the raw material for the paper production, the spark plug, the thermops flask and the 'Ludo' game.
