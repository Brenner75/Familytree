He married at the age of 30 years on a Friday, the 16.10.1870 in Baden_St._Stefan Gindra Anna -, 4 years younger -, a_pub landlady, born in 1845 from Budapest. He was a_waiter and the couple lived in Baden. They had 6 children: Emilia, born in 1869, Karl, born in 1871, Josef, born in 1873, Johanna, born in 1875, Hans, born in 1877 and Michael, born in 1880.

He died at the age of 40 years in the year 1880 36 years earlier than his wife of twisted intestines. Mourning were amongst others his wife Anna, his sister in law Hermine Gindra and Fanny Schwatschek.

He lived in the epoch of Historicism and as a young fellow under the reign of the Emperor Franz-Joseph I of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of his contemporaries of a similar age are Claude Monet, Peter Tschaikowski and Fridrich Nietzsche. During his life there were many wars, amongst them the Sardinian-Austrian war, the Hungarian Revolution and the Sardinian war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during his time are the raw material for the paper production, the light bulb, the telephone and the periodic system of chemical elements.
