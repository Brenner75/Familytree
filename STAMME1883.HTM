Gross Maria was born in the zodiac sign of Saggitarius on a Sunday, the 02.12.1894 in Wien Alt-Simmering as daughter of
  • Spath Josef, born in 1865 from Kirchberg/Wechsel
  • and Pogorsky Viktoria, born in 1870 of Tschechei
Wien (Vienna) is the capital of Austria and you may look at some photos here.

She had 5 bretheren: Irene, born in 1892, Emil, born in 1890, Viktoria, born in 1900, Aloisia, born in 1898 and Alois, born in 1902. She married at the age of 31 years in the year 1925 Gross Stefan -, 4 years older -, a_book binder, born in 1890 from Wien. She was a_book binder (owner) and the couple lived in Wien. About 1 years later had her daughter Gertrude, born in 1926. In the year 1955 she mourned the death of her husband Stefan.

She died at the age of 71 years on a Thursday, the 07.07.1966 in WienV about 11 years later than her husband of stomach cancer. Mourning were amongst others her sister Irene Hawelka, her nephew Franz Ronzer, her nieces Helene Schmidt, Elfriede Werner and Gusti Ehlert as well as further members of the family.

She lived in the epoch of Historicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Franz-Joseph I of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of her contemporaries of a similar age are Agatha Christie and Marlene Dietrich. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Great war, the Second World war and the Korean war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the glider (aeroplane), the 'Ludo' game, Einstein's theory of relativity and the tooth paste.
