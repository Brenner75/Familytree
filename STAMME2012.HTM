Tschech Helmut was born in the zodiac sign of Cancer on a Tuesday, the 07.07.1925 in Breslau. Breslau (Wroclav) is a town in East Prussia (today Poland).

He married at the age of 23 years in the year 1948 Sämmer Liselotte -, 4 years younger -, born in 1930 from München. In the same year had her daughter Ulrike, born in 1948.

He died at the age of 35 years on a Friday, the 05.05.1961 in Muenchen 61 years earlier than his wife of Stroke. Mourning were amongst others his wife Liselotte.

He lived in the epoch of modern times and as a young fellow under the reign of the Emperor Karl I of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of his contemporaries of a similar age are Frank Sinatra, John F. Kennedy and Nelson Mandela. During his life there were the Second World war, the Korean war and the Vietnam war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during his time are the compact camera, the nuclear fission, the computer and the famouse 'curry wurst' in Berlin.
