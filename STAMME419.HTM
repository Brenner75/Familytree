Heinzel Maria was born in the zodiac sign of Gemini on a Wednesday, the 06.06.1764 in Jägerndorf as daughter of
  • Fidler Georg, born in 1735, a_weaver from Jägerndorf
  • and Hattenburger Katharina, born in 1740 of Jägerndorf
Jägerndorf (Krnov) is located 80mi north east of Brno in the Check republic.

She married at the age of 16 years in the year 1780 in Jägerndorf Heinzel Johann Gottfried -, 12 years older -, a_weaver, born in 1751 from Krottendorf. About 14 years later had her daughter Josefa, born in 1794.

The exact date of death is not known, but she died propably at the age of 62 years in the year 1826.

She lived in the epoch of Classicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperess Maria Theresia of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of her contemporaries of a similar age are Maria Antoinette and Madame Tussaud. During her life there were many wars, amongst them Napoleon's wars, the Coalition wars and Napoleon's war on the Iberic Peninsula. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the Homoeopathy and the mouth organ.
