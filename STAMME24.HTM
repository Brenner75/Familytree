He had 7 bretheren: Helene, born in 1875, Franz, born in 1880, Anton, born in 1875, Julie, Anna, born in 1875, Hermine, born in 1875 and Karoline, born in 1889. He married at the age of 27 years in the year 1909 in Hausleiten Fehringer Juliane -, 2 years and 2 months younger -, a_grocery shop owner, born in 1884 from Gaisruck. He was a_milk trader and the couple lived in Gaisruck. They had 2 children: Karl Franz, born in 1910 and Franz, born in 1913.

There is a bit of confusion about the spelling of his forename Karl, which is spelled with a C on his birth certificate, but with a K on his death announcement. Not a lot is known about him, apart from the fact, that he was a milk trader in the vicinity of Gaisruck and he used to distribute the milk with a horse cart. Although the horse was totally blind, it nevertheless found its way for its daily round without help. During the summer months of 1920 he suddenly fell ill with an ear infection. He was only 38 years old and had to be taken to hospital. Before he left, he walked through his house and his garden and said to his 10 year old son, that he knew, that he would never come back again. The infection spread to his brain and killed him shortly afterwards.

He died at the age of 38 years on a Sunday, the 04.07.1920 in Stockerau 41 years earlier than his wife. Mourning were amongst others his wife Juliane, his brother in law Franz Bohacek, his sister in law Barbara Rauscher, his brother Franz, his sister Karoline, his father Karl, his mother Hermine.
