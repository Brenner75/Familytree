Strehlen in Schlesien is located south of Warsaw in East Prussia (today: Poland).

He married at the age of 33 years on a Saturday, the 29.04.1940 Franz Dorothea -, 7 years younger -, a_housewife, born in 1914 from Hohenthan. He was a_soldier and the couple lived in München. They had 4 children: Dieter Max, born in 1940, Gabi, born in 1944, Klaus, born in 1942 and Inge, born in 1948. In the year 1963 he stood by the grave of his child Inge.

He died at the age of 79 years on a Monday, the 01.12.1986 in München of a stroke. Mourning were amongst others his sons Dieter and Klaus, his daughter Gabi, his grandchild Michael, his daughter in law Ulrike.

He lived in the epoch of modern times and as a young fellow under the reign of the Emperor Karl I of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of his contemporaries of a similar age are Ernest Hemingway, Louis Armstrong and Walt Disney. During his life there were many wars, amongst them the Great war, the Second World war and the Korean war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during his time are the tooth paste, the computer, the famouse 'curry wurst' in Berlin and the football boots.
