Gindra Hermine was born in the zodiac sign of Libra on a Tuesday, the 23.10.1849 in Budapest as daughter of
  • Gindra Anton, born in 1810 from Ungarn
  • and Bloszovitz Magdalena, born in 1820 of Budapest
Budapest is the Hungarian capital.

She had 2 bretheren: Anna, born in 1845 and Fanny. She was a_house maid of a bankier and lived in Deutsch_Wagram.

She died at the age of 65 years on a Wednesday, the 07.04.1915 in Deutsch_Wagram of heart valve failure. Mourning were amongst others her sister Anna Binder, her nephew Hans Binder, her niece Emilia Mock as well as further members of the family.

She lived in the epoch of Historicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Franz-Joseph I of Habsburg-Lothringen. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Hungarian Revolution, the Sardinian war and the Italien Independence war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the light bulb, X-rays, the Aspirin pills, and the spark plug.
