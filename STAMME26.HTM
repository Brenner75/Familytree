Spannberg is a little village amongst vinyards in the northern part of Austria (Niederösterreich).

He had 3 bretheren: Ernst, born in 1890, Emma, born in 1893 and Elisabeth, born in 1901. He married at the age of 25 years in the year 1913 in Wien Mock Alice Emilia Anna -, 4 years younger -, a_housewife, born in 1892 from Baden. He was a_teacher and the couple lived in Deutsch_Wagram. They had 2 children: Alice Anna Anna, born in 1916 and Hertha Wilhelmine, born in 1914.

He died at the age of 60 years on a Monday, the 31.01.1949 in Wien 22 years earlier than his wife of kidney failure. Mourning were amongst others his wife Alice, his daughters Alice Brenner and Hertha Seil, his son in law Karl Brenner, his sister Elisabeth Kuhn.
