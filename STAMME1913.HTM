Flamm Theresia was born in 1849 in Wien as daughter of
  • Scholz Karl, born in 1820, a_glove maker from Wien
  • and Kalliwoda Maria, born in 1825, a_washing woman of Österreich
Wien (Vienna) is the capital of Austria and you may look at some photos here.

She had 4 bretheren: Elisabeth, born in 1845, Karl, born in 1846, Anna and Wilhelm, born in 1850. She married at the age of 45 years in the year 1894 Flamm Rudolph -, about 2 years older -, a_physician, born in 1847 from Paris. She was a_ballet dancer and the couple lived in Wien_Mariahilf.

She died at the age of 45 years in the year 1894 maybe by poisoning. Mourning were amongst others her brother in law Johann Mock and Josef Tinz, her nephew Albrecht Mock.

She lived in the epoch of Historicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Franz-Joseph I of Habsburg-Lothringen. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Hungarian Revolution, the Sardinian war and the Italien Independence war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the light bulb, the telephone, the periodic system of chemical elements and the dynamo.
