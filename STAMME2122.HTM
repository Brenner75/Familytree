Zobl Maria was born in 1665 in Prutz. Prutz is a village in Tyrol in the upper Inn valley.

She married at the age of 25 years in the year 1690 Zobl Josef -, about 7 years older -, a_bank clerk, born in 1658 from Prutz. They had 3 children: Christoph, born in 1693, Anna Maria, born in 1691 and Katharina, born in 1695. In the year 1697 she mourned the death of her husband Josef.

She died at the age of 37 years in the year 1702 about 5 years later than her husband.

She lived in the epoch of Baroque and as a young girl under the reign of th Emperor Leopold I of the House of Habsburg. During her life there were the Great Turkish war.
