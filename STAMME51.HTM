Fehringer Theresia was born in the zodiac sign of Scorpio on a Wednesday, the 17.11.1847 in Niederrussbach as daughter of
  • Haslinger Johann, born in 1813, a_labourer from Niederrussbach
  • and Knoll Theresia, born in 1818 of Niederrussbach
Niederrussbach is a tiny village in Lower Austria.

She married at the age of 21 years in the year 1868 in Hausleiten Fehringer Franz -, 3 years and 4 months older -, a_sheperd, born in 1844 from Gettsdorf. The couple lived in Gaisruck. They had 8 children: Juliane, born in 1884, Franz, born in 1880, Karl, born in 1878, Barbara, born in 1870, Therese, born in 1872, Marie, born in 1874, Katharina, born in 1888 and Anna, born in 1876.

She died at the age of 65 years on a Wednesday, the 22.10.1913 in Gaisruck 6 years earlier than her husband of water in the heart. Mourning were amongst others her husband Franz, her daughters Juliane Brenner and Barbara Rauscher, her son in law Carl Brenner.

She lived in the epoch of Historicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Franz-Joseph I of Habsburg-Lothringen. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Sardinian-Austrian war, the Hungarian Revolution and the Sardinian war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the light bulb, the dynamo, Blue Jeans and the bakteriology.
